Mission



LK Global S.A. is an international cargo agent specialised in ocean and air transport from and to Far East, with strong presence in all the Foreign Trade lanes...


We have a highly skilled management with wide knowledge of the local and international market, a reliable work group able to handle all kinds of operations, and full support of an international agents network that allows us to operate comfortably and safely in any country where we are required to provide import or export services.


We offer professional advice regarding all transport aspects and the appropriate cargo handling to make sure that the cargo arrives at destination on time, every time, being this our corporate commitment, and that of all our collaborators, who offer a quality service with maximum efficiency.


Our strategy is to link our operations to take advantage of the sinergies between all the involved parties, providing added value in all our services. In this way, we have achieved quite positive results by offering first class services, complete reliability, high technical skills and efficient cost structures.


We are very pleased to offer you all the virtues of our Organisation.
